Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working together

What makes B2B copywriting different from B2C?

B2B copywriting addresses longer sales cycles, multiple decision-makers, and complex product offerings. It focuses on ROI, business outcomes, and building trust through expertise rather than emotion-driven impulse purchases. The copy speaks to rational business needs while maintaining persuasive elements that drive action.

How much does B2B copywriting cost?

B2B copywriting projects typically range from £2,000 to £10,000 depending on scope and complexity:

  • Website copy: £3,500-£8,000 (2-4 weeks)
  • Landing pages: £1,200-£2,500 (1-2 weeks)
  • Email campaigns: £800-£3,000 (1-2 weeks)
  • Ad copy: £500-£1,500 (3-5 days)

What types of brands do you work with?

I specialize in feel-good brands—companies making a positive impact in health, wellness, sustainability, and lifestyle. Think Loop Earplugs (£120M+ e-commerce), Huel (nutrition), F1 Arcade (experiences), Navan (B2B SaaS), and Cotswold Outdoor (sustainability-focused retail). If you're building something people genuinely want to support, we'll probably get along.

How long does a copywriting project take?

Most B2B copywriting projects take 2-4 weeks from brief to delivery. This includes:

  • Research and strategy development (3-5 days)
  • Initial drafts (5-7 days)
  • Client feedback and revisions (3-5 days)
  • Final delivery and optimization (2-3 days)

Rush projects can be accommodated with adjusted timelines and pricing.

Do you offer SEO copywriting services?

Yes, all copywriting services include SEO optimization as standard. This includes keyword research, search intent analysis, meta tag optimization, and structured content that performs well in both traditional search engines (Google) and AI-powered search (ChatGPT, Perplexity). The goal is copy that ranks well AND converts visitors into customers.

What's your process for new clients?

The process starts with a discovery call to understand your business, audience, and goals. Then we move through research, strategy, drafting, feedback, and delivery. You'll have direct access throughout, with regular check-ins and unlimited rounds of revisions within the agreed scope. The focus is on creating copy that works, not just looks good.

Can you help with existing copy that isn't converting?

Absolutely. Many clients come to us with copy that technically explains what they do, but doesn't drive action. We audit your existing content, identify what's not working, and rewrite it with a focus on clarity, persuasion, and conversion. Often, small strategic changes to messaging and structure can dramatically improve results.
